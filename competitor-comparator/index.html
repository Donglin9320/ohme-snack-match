Comparison method

Read by lane.

A product can be useful as a direct format comparison or as a broader packaging benchmark. The page labels the role rather than implying every snack is a direct substitute.

Source hygiene

What the links mean.

The comparator is intentionally transparent about the limits of public web research.

Current, not permanent

Retail prices and availability can change. The linked page is the current reference point.

Signal, not sales proof

A public product page confirms an offer and its claims; it does not prove exact category sales.

Role-labelled comparisons

Closest formats and broader packaging references are separated so the comparison remains clear.
