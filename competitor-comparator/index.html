OHME brand context

Why OHME reads differently.

The comparator becomes more useful when public listings are read against OHME's own visible product story—not against generic “healthy snack” language.

01 · Ingredient cue

Real fruit is the first reading lens.

Use public ingredient and product pages to confirm the claim; do not infer it from package colour alone.

02 · Claim cue

Claims need a simple hierarchy.

The public page compares how claims are presented, not whether one product is healthier than another.

03 · Process cue

Freeze-dried is a product-form signal.

That process separates the closest-format lane from bars, granola bites, and candy references.

Source hygiene

Transparent by design.

The value of a public comparator is not certainty. It is a clear trail back to the page where each signal was observed.

Current, not permanent

Retail prices, stock, pack formats, and product pages change. Always reopen the linked source before quoting a current offer.

Signal, not sales proof

A public listing confirms that an offer or claim is shown. It does not prove demand, conversion, or category leadership.

Images identify listings

Product images help visitors recognize the listing. They are not used to infer dimensions, volume, or market performance.
